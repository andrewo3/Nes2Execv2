The two ROMs are the exact same game with changes for emulator compatibility.
Test both to see which one would work for your emulator.

To play this game on your home computer, we recommend using FCEUX and opening the NES file in the 'Default ROM' folder.

Description:

This game is not for the faint of heart!

In ‘PROTO DERE .NES’, you play as a character trying to escape a burning underground mine.
Use your athletic prowess to jump over fires and climb over ladders to reach the exit.
From the ashes of dread, a new life is born.

High Quality Graphics
Four Channel Sound
Unique Characters
Endless Fun
Do you have what it takes to survive? Find out in PROTO DERE .NES!
